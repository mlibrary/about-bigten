A﻿s a unique collaboration between the libraries and university presses of the Big Ten Academic Alliance, Big Ten Open Books is naturally committed to created ebook collections that aspire to the highest standards of discoverability, accessibility, durability, and flexibility. Here is how the collection lives our shared values:

* **D﻿iscoverability:** MARC records for the books in Big Ten Open Books collections are prepared by [Backstage Library Works](https://www.bslw.com/history-of-backstage-library-works/)[](https://www.bslw.com/history-of-backstage-library-works/)﻿ and are made available when each collection is launched. As well as being available for direct download, they are distributed via OCLC. Big Ten Open Books collections are
